Introduction

This website is an information guide that will be useful to the viewer and those who may need help understanding the Coronavirus disease, also known as COVID-19. What is this disease? It is an infectious disease caused by the SARS-CoV-2 virus that someone can experience mild to moderate respiratory illness depending on the person. Anyone can get the disease and even get seriously ill or die if left untreated.
